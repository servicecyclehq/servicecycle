## How 70B is structured (confirmed)
- **Chapter 9 / Table 9.2.2** = the interval table: per product line, per SCOPE OF WORK (visual inspection / cleaning / lubrication / mechanical servicing / electrical testing / special), the months for Condition 1/2/3. (Already encoded + verified.)
- **Each equipment chapter (11-38)** = the LIST of specific tasks within each scope, plus occasional frequency OVERRIDES. Section .2 = "Frequency of Maintenance" (defers to Ch 9 unless overridden); section .3 = "Periodic Maintenance Procedures" (the task tables). Test "Type" 1/1A/2/2A = online/offline, standard/enhanced (per Section 8.3).
